Genre: Rock, Indie

Coldplay is a globally renowned British rock band formed in London in 1996 (officially 1997/1998 in various accounts) by Chris Martin (vocals/piano), Jonny Buckland (guitar), Guy Berryman (bass), and Will Champion (drums).
